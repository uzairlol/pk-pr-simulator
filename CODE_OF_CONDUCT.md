# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience, or orientation.
## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior include empathy, kindness, respect for diverse viewpoints, and constructive criticism.
Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting comments, and non-consensual sharing of private information.
## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing these standards and taking corrective action against unacceptable behavior.
